The Archive

A Decade of Excellence

2010

The Inception

Glow Salon opens its doors as a boutique 3-chair studio, focusing entirely on bespoke, intimate hair care experiences and personal connections.

2013

The Artisan Collective

We expanded our team, bringing in master stylists from Paris and Milan to establish an unmatched standard for architectural cutting and styling.

2015

The Spa Expansion

Responding to client demand for holistic wellness, we acquired the adjoining property and launched our award-winning luxury spa wing.

2019

Global Recognition

Awarded "Best Luxury Spa" by Vogue Lifestyle and became the flagship exclusive partner for prestigious brands like Kérastase and Valmont.

2021

The Sustainability Pledge

We transitioned to 100% cruelty-free, sustainable product lines and implemented a zero-waste policy across all our styling stations.

2024

The Private Suite

Introduced the ultra-exclusive VIP Private Suites, setting a new global standard for discreet, high-end salon and spa experiences.
